I go by the name of Lintnaya.

Currently doing pixel art and animation for video games and stuff.

I like egg dishes, orange and avocado juice, and also cheese. Love visiting new places but prefer to stay inside.

Life is miserable and joyful at the same time, yet I prefer to sleep. When I die, I want to reincarnate as a tomato, live as red as I can be.

This site was created to share my artwork and other thing(s) in the shape of images, text, HTML, CSS, and a little bit of javascript.

Stuff in this site are made by me, unless I stated otherwise. Sharing is allowed with credit.

Make my heart flutter. Make it scream as loud as possible. Make it bleed in grotesque.

Stupid choice of words. But as the world keeps spinning, I'm still here wondering.

I update this site whenever I want. Add some artwork(s), small changes, or new page(s) as some kind of experiment.

In order to know, I need to learn. In order to live, I need to suffer.

This website is powered by my rusty brain and 1/4 of my heart.
